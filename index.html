How it works

Three seconds to the truth about a passport.

Hold a passport against your iPhone. The app reads the chip, verifies the signature chain against ICAO PKD trust anchors, and tells you whether the document is authentic — using exactly the same cryptographic logic as customs hardware.

1

Paste the MRZ

Enter the 88-character machine readable zone from the passport photo page. Or scan it with the camera — OCR runs on-device, no images leave your iPhone.

2

Tap the chip

Hold the passport at the top edge of your iPhone. The app opens a BAC or PACE secure channel with the embedded contactless chip.

3

See the verdict

In under 15 seconds: green INTEGRITY VERIFIED with holder identity, or red VERIFICATION FAILED with no personal information exposed.
